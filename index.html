Our Medicines

Quality pharmaceutical products for your healthcare needs

Diabetes Medications

Comprehensive range of oral anti-diabetic medications to help manage blood glucose levels effectively and prevent complications.

Insulin

Premium quality insulin products available in various formulations including rapid-acting, long-acting, and intermediate options for diabetes management.

Antihypertensives

Effective medications to control high blood pressure including ACE inhibitors, ARBs, calcium channel blockers, and beta-blockers.

Proton Pump Inhibitors

Advanced medications that reduce stomach acid production, effectively treating conditions like GERD, heartburn, and stomach ulcers.

Cholesterol Medications

Statins and other lipid-lowering therapies to help manage cholesterol levels and reduce the risk of cardiovascular disease.

Thyroid Replacement

Precise thyroid hormone replacement therapies to treat hypothyroidism and help restore normal metabolic functions.

Anti-Allergics

Effective antihistamines and other anti-allergy medications to provide relief from seasonal allergies, hay fever, and allergic reactions.

Anti-Fungal

Potent anti-fungal treatments available in various forms including creams, tablets, and solutions to treat fungal infections effectively.

Cough Syrups

Range of formulations to soothe throat irritation, suppress coughs, and provide relief from symptoms of colds and flu.
