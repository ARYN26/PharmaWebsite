Health Education & Pharmacy Tips

Essential health information from our DOH certified pharmacist

Diabetes Management

Proper storage of insulin is crucial - keep refrigerated between 2-8°C. Never freeze insulin. Monitor blood glucose levels regularly and take medications as prescribed by your healthcare provider.

Diabetes Care

Blood Pressure Control

Take blood pressure medications at the same time daily. Avoid skipping doses. Monitor your blood pressure regularly and maintain a healthy diet low in sodium and high in potassium.

Hypertension

Medication Timing

Never skip doses or stop medications abruptly. Use pill organizers to track your medications. If you miss a dose, consult your pharmacist about the best course of action.

Medication Safety

Proper Storage

Store medications in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Check expiration dates regularly. Keep medications in original containers with labels for proper identification.
